About me

Guilherme Iecker Ricardo received his B.Eng. degree in Computer Engineering in 2016 and M.Sc. degree in Computer Science in 2018 both from Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro (Brazil). In 2021, he received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Université Côte d'Azur (France) under the supervision of Prof. Giovanni Neglia (Inria) and Prof. Thrasyvoulos Spyropoulos (EURECOM). As of January 2022, he joined Orange Group as a postdoctoral researcher and is currently part of the Mathematical Models for Optimization and peRformance Evalualtion (MORE) research department at Orange Labs (France) where he works on resource allocation problems for future networks.
